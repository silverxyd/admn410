History

Our family has been apart of this Community and have lived in Barrington for over 6 generations, and having to drive long distances to get pet supplies, helped us make our decision to open a local pet store. We have everything you need to take care of your pets, including food, treats, toys, litter, supplies, medicine, chicken coops, rabbit hutches, dog houses, cages and Wild Bird Seed! We also work with the local animal shelters and rescues to help adopt out animals, So keep checking back in our store to see who's up for adoption next!

Why Choose Us

We know how important it is to provide the best quality of life for your pets. It is our mission to provide you with a friendly, helpful, and knowledgeable shopping experience so that you may extend the lives of your furry (and scaly) friends.
